A local from the Kainai Nation, born in Calgary, grew up in Cardston, very quaint town south near the border. Played basketball throughout my years attending school, my favorite sport, never went beyond high school for athletics.
Have an older brother, who is a big inspiration in my life and always wanted to be tall and athletic like him, also have three younger brothers.

Started out with multimedia when my family purchased our first computer as a family, immediately got hooked on MS paint, decided from then on pursue creative endeavours in multimedia, found my start in the multi-media from a coding summer camp attended during the covid years of lockdown.
Presently enrolled in the Multi-media production program at Lethbridge Polytechnic and part-time public servant as Business Expertise Advisor for Employment and Social Development of Canada.

Passionate about digital design, graphic, website and user interface design are the priority interests, avid gamer who enjoys role-playing games with a focus on storytelling, other interests, favorite band is Pearl Jam and love pastel colours.
Featured in the first issue of “Ersa Minor” school magazine at the Lethbridge Polytechnic as well, and contributed to the Indigenous designed school uniforms.
