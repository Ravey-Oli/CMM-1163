Economy and Services

A major banking hub during the renaissance, utilizing the Flourin as it's currency, now being known for its high-end fashion and luxurious offerings.

Founding place to some of the largest brands in the world, Gucci, Prada, Roberto Cavalli and Chanel, most moving headquarters to Milan, while still having offices and stores within the city.

Being situated in the Tuscany province of Italy, renowned for its wineries and vineyards producing some of the most prestigious available, while growing some of the finest Italian produce in the land.

Modern industry consisting of small-scale textile operations and modern technological development, but largely being dependent on tourism as a whole for the cities economy.
