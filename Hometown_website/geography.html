Geography of Florence

Florence is situated on the Arno River basin, within the picturesque Senese Clavey Hills of Tuscany Italy, around 300km north of Italy's capital of Rome.

Surrounded by grand villas, opulent orchids, fantastic farms and beautiful vineyards magnificent Italian countryside is a sight to behold for the nature-oriented connoisseur.

The temperature is a very mild mediterranean sub-tropical climate, with hot humid summers with sparse rain and cooler damp winters with the occasional snowfall.

Average summer temps are around 30 degrees Celsius, with winter temperatures being about 10 degrees Celsius on average.
